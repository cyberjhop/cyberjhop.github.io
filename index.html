About Me

Hello! I'm a passionate developer

With over 7 years of experience in the tech and creative space, I help bring ideas to life using a combination of modern tools, clean workflows, and creative execution. I’m an IT Specialist and former educator with a strong foundation in technology, having taught programming and guided individuals in building real, practical skills.

My work goes beyond development. I create responsive websites, design compelling graphics, produce engaging videos, and craft creative ads that connect with audiences. I’m comfortable working across a wide range of tools and platforms in today’s digital market, allowing me to adapt quickly and deliver solutions that are both functional and visually impactful.

When I’m not working, you’ll likely find me experimenting with new creative ideas or enjoying a good cup of coffee. Let’s collaborate and build something meaningful.
